About Remya ma'am

A teacher who has seen what actually works.

Remya Santhosh spent over two decades teaching Zoology in the classroom before retiring — and has since kept teaching, one home at a time. That experience shows up in the small things: knowing where students usually get stuck, which explanations land, and how to keep a subject from feeling like a wall of things to memorise.

She now takes home tuition for students in Class 8 and above, covering Zoology for students above the grade of 10th and Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Maths— helping students build real understanding, not just exam answers.
